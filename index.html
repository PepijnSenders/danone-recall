What happened?

Cereulide, a heat-stable toxin from Bacillus cereus, was found in baby formula. The toxin was traced to contaminated ARA oil (an omega-6 ingredient). It can cause vomiting, cramps, and dehydration in infants. It is not destroyed by boiling water.

What should I do?

If your batch matches, stop using the product immediately. Return it to the store for a full refund. If your baby has symptoms (vomiting, diarrhea), contact your doctor. See EFSA advisory for details.
